Dare To Share.

Donate your excess meal swipes.

We, the undersigned, are students at The University of Pittsburgh that wish to address student food insecurity on campus with a donation program that will allow students to donate meal swipes to other students in need.

More than 36% of students on college campuses across the United States suffer from food insecurity. Conversely, many students with dining plans often experience surplus meals at the end of each term, that they will not use. Sharing Excess seeks to work with university administration and food service providers to facilitate meal swipe donations that will prove more effective than a traditional on-campus food pantry.

With our donation program, we intend to allow students to transfer a limited number of their excess meal swipes to other students who have indicated a need for support. These students may then receive these meals anonymously through their existing dining accounts, or designated meal cards.

We ask you to join us in helping to end hunger on college campuses and communities across America. Your signature will help bring Sharing Excess to The University of Pittsburgh to facilitate meal swipe donations.
